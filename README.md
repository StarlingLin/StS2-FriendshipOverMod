3. # 杀戮尖塔2：友尽对决模组
   
   一个面向 **Slay the Spire 2 多人模式** 的整活向模组。
   
   卡图与创意来自  
   [BiliBili @ 一只冰茄子](https://www.bilibili.com/video/BV1SqX4B4Eiq/)
   
   ## 前置需求
   
   使用本模组前，需要先安装（我已经一起打包在release里了）：
   
   - **BaseLib v0.2.0**
   
   ## 安装方式
   
   1. 从Release中下载mods.zip。
   2. 将解压出来的两个文件夹（一个本模组，一个BaseLib v0.2.0）移动至Slay the Spire根目录下的mods文件夹中。

   # StS2-FriendshipOverMod
   
   A chaos-themed mod for **Slay the Spire 2 multiplayer**.
   
   Card art and some ideas are based on  
   [BiliBili @ 一只冰茄子](https://www.bilibili.com/video/BV1SqX4B4Eiq/)
   
   ## Requirements
   
   Before using this mod, please install the following dependency  
   (already included in the release package):
   
   - **BaseLib v0.2.0**
   
   ## Installation
   
   1. Download `mods.zip` from the Release page.
   2. Extract the archive and move the two folders  
      (this mod and **BaseLib v0.2.0**)  
      into the `mods` folder under your Slay the Spire root directory.
   
   ## Current Implemented Cards
   
   ### 1. Self-Destruct
   - 3 Energy
   - Rare
   - Attack
   - Defect card
   - Multiplayer-exclusive
   
   Effect:
   - Lose HP
   - Deal heavy damage to all enemies
   - Also deal damage to all allies
   
   Upgraded:
   - Reduced self-damage
   - Increased damage to all enemies
   
   ---
   
   ### 2. Chaos Drain
   - 2 Energy
   - Uncommon
   - Power
   - Silent card
   - Multiplayer-exclusive
   
   Effect:
   - A random ally loses Strength and Dexterity
   - You gain more Strength and Dexterity
   
   Upgraded:
   - The ally loses less Strength and Dexterity
   
   ---
   
   ### 3. Castling
   - 2 Energy
   - Uncommon
   - Skill
   - Regent card
   - Multiplayer-exclusive
   
   Effect:
   - Swap your Block with the chosen ally's Block
   
   Upgraded:
   - After swapping Block, refund half of that ally's original Block
   
   ---
   
   ### 4. Collector
   - 2 Energy
   - Rare
   - Attack
   - Multiplayer-exclusive
   
   Effect:
   - Deal damage
   - On Fatal, seize the **Collector** state and mark other allies as **Plundered**
   - Gold rewards gained by plundered allies during this combat are transferred to the Collector
   - Exhaust
   
   Upgraded:
   - Increased damage
   - Gains Innate
   
   ---
   
   ### 5. Spread Fire
   - 1 Energy
   - Rare
   - Skill
   - Colorless
   - Multiplayer-exclusive
   
   Effect:
   - Gain a large amount of Block
   - All allies gain 1 Vulnerable
   - Exhaust
   
   Upgraded:
   - Cost becomes 0
   
   ---
   
   ### 6. One Step Ahead
   - 1 Energy
   - Rare
   - Skill
   - Colorless
   - Multiplayer-exclusive
   
   Effect:
   - Draw cards
   - Shuffle either a **Slimed** or **Dazed** into an ally's draw pile
   
   Upgraded:
   - Draw more cards
